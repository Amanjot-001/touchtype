Key Features

Touch Typing

You can practice your typing speed and enhance your abilities.
Whether you're a beginner or an experienced typist, our site provides a variety of materials to suit your needs.

Learn syllabus by typing

Our site offers a unique approach to learning, instead of just reading you can actively engage with the material by typing.
This improves your typing speed and accuracy in the process.
With our platform, learning college syllabus has never been more interactive or effective.

Customizable typing text

You can select from a variety of pre-given notes to type out, or you can choose any section of the notes and start typing.
With our customizable note typing practice feature, you can take control of your learning and achieve better results.

Keep track with others

With our platform, you can see how you stack up against your classmates in terms of typing speed and accuracy.
Our ranking system allows you to track your progress and see where you stand relative to your peers. This can be a great motivator to improve your typing skills and outperform your classmates.
